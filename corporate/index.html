Where Mira florals already live

Different industries move at different rhythms. But the first impression a space leaves — that almost always comes from the same place.

  • Boutique hotels & resorts Lobbies · Suites · Public spaces First impressions decide whether someone wants to stay. Flowers here aren't decoration — they're part of the space's character.
  • Medical aesthetics & beauty clinics Waiting rooms · Consult rooms · Spa Before anyone relaxes, they first decide whether they feel safe. Clean, restrained, well-proportioned flowers help a room settle faster.
  • Law, accounting, finance offices Reception · Partner offices The room doesn't need to say much. It just can't have a single thing out of place.
  • Postpartum & maternal care centers Mother and infant spaces Air, light, scent, the small details — all of it is felt more sharply here. Flowers help the room feel lighter, softer.
  • Galleries & design studios Exhibition spaces · Creative spaces Flowers don't need to take the lead here — but they have to live naturally alongside everything else in the room.
  • Tech offices Open work areas · Common spaces Highly rational spaces need a small touch of warmth most of all. Not decoration — the kind of detail that makes people want to stay, and want to work.
  • The principal's office This is a room where judgment is made. The way it presents — before a single word is spoken — already says everything.

Common questions

What's included in the $99 assessment?

Two weeks · one small on-site floral installation (a reception desk or front area) · our judgment on style, size, and placement. Only two to three assessments accepted each quarter.

Do I have to sign a contract after the assessment?

No. The two weeks is the test — you see what florals look like in your space, and we share our judgment on whether they fit. If they don't, you walk away with that clarity.

What's the starting monthly cost?

From $129/week. Final pricing depends on the space, the number of points, the specification, and the refresh frequency — we'll discuss it openly during the assessment and present a transparent quote.

Can I cancel the contract?

Three-month minimum. After that, adjust or cancel month to month with 48 hours' notice.

Can we add florals for holidays?

Yes. Before every major holiday we reach out with options for the holiday edition, quoted separately.

Who handles the weekly swap?

We do — at a fixed weekly window agreed with your team.

What if an arrangement looks tired mid-week?

If it's a stem-quality issue, we replace at no charge. For special events or high-profile visits where peak presentation matters, mid-week refresh can be arranged on request — pricing depends on the situation.

What's your refund policy?

Subscription changes, pauses, or cancellations require 48 hours' notice. Full terms: Service & Replacement Policy →
