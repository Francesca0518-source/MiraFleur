Common questions

What's included in the $99 assessment?

Two weeks of on-site observation, plus four formal deliverables (Space Observation Report / Long-Term Floral Rhythm / Seasonal Palette Plan / Two-Week Floral Record). Only 2–3 assessments accepted each quarter.

Do I have to sign a contract after the assessment?

No. The assessment stands on its own — even if you choose not to continue, what you receive is a complete, usable floral plan.

What's the starting monthly cost?

From $129/week. Final pricing depends on space, points, specification, and refresh frequency. We'll discuss it openly during the assessment and give you a transparent quote.

Can I cancel the contract?

Three-month minimum. After that, adjust or cancel month to month with 48 hours' notice.

Can we add florals for holidays?

Yes. We reach out before each major holiday with options for holiday editions (quoted separately).

Who handles the weekly swap?

We do — at a fixed weekly window agreed with your team.

What if an arrangement looks tired mid-week?

If it's a stem quality issue, we replace at no charge. For special events or high-profile visits where you want peak presentation, mid-week refresh can be arranged on request — pricing depends on the situation.

What's your refund policy?

Subscription changes, pauses, or cancellations require 48 hours' notice. Full terms: Service & Replacement Policy →
