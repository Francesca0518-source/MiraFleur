Care

When the flowers arrive, cut 1–2 cm from each stem at an angle and place them in fresh water. Refresh every two days.

Keep them out of direct sun, away from heat and fruit. With care, most blooms hold for 5–7 days; hydrangeas and garden roses can carry on for ten days or more.

Common questions

How does The Limited Edition work?

One palette per week, three sizes ($49 / $99 / $159). Wednesday cutoff, Friday pickup. Major holidays carry their own editions.

Where is pickup?

An Irvine pickup point. The exact address is shared with the order confirmation by email or text. Same window every Friday.

Is delivery available?

Yes. Delivery from $15, calculated by distance. Select it at checkout.

Payment terms?

The Limited Edition is pre-ordered and paid in full at checkout. Once the cutoff passes, orders cannot be cancelled or modified.

Can I order from a previous week?

No. Each week's edition runs that week only — never repeated. Once it's gone, it's gone.

What's your refund policy?

The Limited Edition is pre-ordered and perishable, so we don't accept returns once an arrangement has been delivered. If there's a freshness concern on arrival, reach out within 12 hours — we'll offer a partial refresh, adjustment, or replacement of selected elements as the situation calls for.
