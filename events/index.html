Common questions

What is included in event design?

A site visit, a written design direction with palette and proportion notes, sourcing of seasonal stems, full installation on the day, and post-event breakdown. Larger productions can include vendor coordination with planners, photographers, and venues.

What is the minimum, and how is pricing structured?

Event work begins at $2,500. Pricing is built around scope — flower volume, structural builds, install crew, transport — and is presented as a single transparent quote. No hidden fees, no per-stem padding.

How far in advance should we book?

Most events: two to four weeks. Weddings and large brand activations: six to twelve weeks for full design development. Tight timelines are accepted when calendar allows, with a rush fee disclosed upfront.

Do you travel beyond Orange County?

Yes. We regularly work across Orange County and Greater Los Angeles. Destinations beyond Southern California are considered case by case for the right project.
