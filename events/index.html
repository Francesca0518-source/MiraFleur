Common questions

What's the starting price?

From $500. Final pricing depends on scale, stem volume, point count, and vessel selection. Presented as a single transparent quote — no hidden fees.

How large an event can you take on?

Private events under 50 guests are our sweet spot. Larger events are taken on case by case, based on calendar and team capacity.

Where do you serve?

Currently across Orange County. Destinations beyond OC are considered case by case for the right project.

Do you handle breakdown?

Standard service does not include breakdown — vessels and stems are yours to keep. Breakdown available on separate quote if needed.

Can we change the design on the day?

Not on the day. Stems and vessels are sourced a week before the event. Anything to adjust, please raise during the design phase.

What about lighting, seating, and venue layout?

Tell us about the venue's existing setup during the design phase — our florals coordinate with what's already there. We don't provide lighting or seating directly, but coordinate with planners and venue staff.

Payment terms?

50% deposit on design confirmation. Balance plus delivery fee due before delivery.

Deposit refund policy?

Stems begin sourcing once the deposit is paid; deposits are non-refundable. Date changes possible up to 7 days before the event, subject to availability. Within 7 days, deposit is forfeited.

How far in advance should we book?

Most events: 2–4 weeks. Larger events or weddings: 6–12 weeks for full design development. Tight timelines accepted when calendar allows; rush fees disclosed upfront.

What's your refund policy?

As event florals are custom and perishable, we do not accept returns once arrangements have been delivered. We confirm every installation detail with you before the event; any stem issues at the venue are handled immediately on site. Full terms: Service & Replacement Policy →
