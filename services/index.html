Services

Four ways to work with Mira Fleur.

01 · Starting at $180

Custom Arrangements

One-of-a-kind bouquets and installations, designed around your occasion, color palette, and the person receiving them. Every custom arrangement begins with a short conversation — the occasion, the recipient, the feeling. From there, we propose a design direction, a flower palette, and a transparent quote. Once confirmed, the piece is hand-crafted on the day of delivery and driven to the recipient in person.

Typical sizes range from hand-tied bouquets and vase arrangements to dining-table centerpieces and entryway statements. We recommend booking three days ahead. Same-day is sometimes possible for Irvine and Newport Coast addresses when inquired before 12:00 PM PT — a rush fee applies.

02 · One week minimum · Quote per event

Event Florals

Florals for the occasions that hold a room together — birthdays, proposals, baby showers, anniversary dinners, intimate weddings. Every event engagement begins with a written brief from you (the request form is the cleanest way to share it), followed by a design direction and quote. On the day, we install on site and return for teardown if the venue requires it.

Lead time: one week minimum for everyday events; three to six weeks preferred for intimate weddings, and longer for peak season (May–October). We are not currently taking on full-scale wedding installations — for those, we're happy to refer trusted partners.

Inquire about Event Florals →

03 · Limited to 2–3 spaces per quarter

Mira Space Florals

An ongoing relationship with a single space — a hotel lobby, an executive office, a gallery, a retail interior — built around weekly or biweekly florals that change with the season. Front-of-house arrangements, reception pieces, or full-room compositions, designed in conversation with the architecture and the people who pass through it.

Every space partnership begins with a Mira Space Preview — a two-week observation. A site visit, time spent in the space at different hours, and a written report on palette, scale, cadence, and a recommended long-term direction. From there, you decide whether to proceed. The Preview is intentionally slow; the work that follows is meant to last.

Standing engagements begin at one quarter (three months). To keep the work attentive, we accept only two to three new space partners per quarter.

Inquire about a Space Preview →

04 · Starting at $220 / week

Weekly Subscriptions

Fresh florals delivered to your home on a recurring schedule — weekly, biweekly, or monthly. Each delivery is a different composition, designed to the season and to the aesthetic we've established with you over time. Palette preferences, vessels, and placement are saved on file after your onboarding consultation. Vacation pauses and vase exchange are included.

This is our most personal service, and it's limited to a small number of private clients. First delivery typically within 2 weeks of the onboarding conversation.
