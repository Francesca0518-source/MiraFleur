Services

Four ways to work with Mira Fleur.

01 · Starting at $180

Custom Arrangements

One-of-a-kind bouquets and installations, designed around your occasion, color palette, and the person receiving them. Every custom arrangement begins with a short conversation — the occasion, the recipient, the feeling. From there, we propose a design direction, a flower palette, and a transparent quote. Once confirmed, the piece is hand-crafted on the day of delivery and driven to the recipient in person.

Typical sizes range from hand-tied bouquets and vase arrangements to dining-table centerpieces and entryway statements. Lead time of 48 hours is ideal; same-day is often possible for Orange County addresses inquired before 12:00 PM PT.

02 · Starting at $2,500

Event & Wedding Florals

Full-service floral design for weddings, proposals, private parties, and corporate events. Every event engagement begins with a consultation — in person at our Newport Coast atelier, at your venue, or by phone — followed by a written design deck and mood board for approval. On the day, our team installs on site and returns for teardown.

Pricing tiers: Intimate events from $2,500. Full-day weddings from $8,000. Luxury multi-venue events are priced on application. For peak season (May–October), we recommend booking at least 3 months in advance; 6 months for full-day weddings.

03 · Starting at $450 / delivery

Corporate Accounts

Florals in a corporate setting aren't decoration — they're a signal. We design for brand openings, executive offices, private client dinners, press events, and year-round executive gifting programs across Irvine and surrounding Orange County. We invoice monthly for recurring clients and offer dedicated account management for brand activation and gifting programs.

Single deliveries start at $450. Recurring programs are priced quarterly based on cadence, scale, and palette. Lead time: 72 hours preferred for single deliveries; recurring programs are planned one quarter ahead.

Inquire about Corporate Accounts →

04 · Starting at $220 / week

Weekly Subscriptions

Fresh florals delivered to your home or office on a recurring schedule — weekly, biweekly, or monthly. Each delivery is a different composition, designed to the season and to the aesthetic we've established with you over time. Palette preferences, vessels, and placement are saved on file after your onboarding consultation. Vacation pauses and vase exchange are included.

This is our most personal service, and it's limited to a small number of private clients. First delivery typically within 2 weeks of the onboarding conversation.
