Common questions

How long does it take to fulfill an order?

Most gifts ship same-day if ordered by 12 PM PT in OC. Outside OC or for fully custom palettes, we usually need 2–3 days.

Can you change the color palette?

Yes — palette is one of the first things we discuss. Tell us a color you want or a color to avoid, and we work from there.

Is there a minimum?

$98 minimum for a single arrangement. Most signature gifts are $180–$320.
