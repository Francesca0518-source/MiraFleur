Ordering & Delivery

How far in advance should I place an order?

For custom arrangements, 48 hours is ideal, though we accommodate same-day requests for Orange County addresses when inquiries arrive before 12:00 PM PT. For weddings and large events, we recommend 6 weeks minimum; 3–6 months for peak season.

Where do you deliver?

For single-piece gift deliveries, we stay within an Irvine-centered Orange County footprint: Irvine (our home base), Newport Coast, Newport Beach, Corona del Mar, Costa Mesa, Tustin, Laguna Beach, Laguna Niguel, Aliso Viejo, and Lake Forest. For weddings, corporate events, and large installations, we travel farther — including Los Angeles and eastern OC — on a case-by-case basis.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, for most Orange County addresses when the inquiry and confirmation happen before 12:00 PM Pacific Time. Same-day is not guaranteed — we'll confirm availability within 30 minutes.

What is your minimum order?

Custom arrangements start at $180. Event work begins at $2,500.

About the Work

Can I request specific flowers?

Yes, though we work with what is genuinely beautiful in any given week. If a requested flower is not in peak condition, we'll let you know and propose alternatives with the same feeling.

Will my arrangement look exactly like a photo I saw?

No two Mira Fleur arrangements are ever identical. We design each piece individually — so while we can honor a reference photo's mood, palette, and composition, the final result will be one of a kind.

Do you handle Chinese cultural traditions and color customs?

Yes. We are experienced with Chinese wedding, memorial, and milestone traditions, including color symbolism, appropriate flowers for each occasion, and ceremonial formats. Please mention any traditions or customs in your inquiry.

Can you work to a brief for a photoshoot or brand activation?

Yes. We regularly collaborate with photographers, stylists, and brands. Share the brief and we'll respond with a design direction.

Logistics & Care

How do I keep my arrangement fresh?

We include a care card with every delivery. In general: keep flowers out of direct sunlight, away from air vents and fruit, change water every two days, and trim stems at an angle every few days.

Can I order flowers anonymously?

Yes. We handle card messaging, anonymity, and delivery timing with full discretion.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Yes. We invoice monthly for recurring corporate clients and offer dedicated account management for brand activation and executive gifting programs.

Do you ship nationally?

Not currently. Mira Fleur is a hand-delivered local service. Every arrangement is built on the day of delivery and driven to the recipient — shipping would compromise both.
