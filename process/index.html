How We Work

A considered process, kept simple.

01

Inquire

Share the occasion, date, recipient, and a sense of the feeling you're after. A simple form, or a text — whichever is easier. We read every inquiry personally and reply the same business day.

02

Consult

We respond within the same business day with a design direction, flower palette, and a transparent quote. For weddings and events, this includes a mood board and a conversation — by phone, by text, or at our Newport Coast atelier.

03

Create

Once confirmed, your arrangement is hand-crafted by our atelier on the day of delivery — never earlier. We send you a photo of the finished piece before it leaves the studio, so you know exactly what's arriving.

04

Deliver

Hand-delivered across Irvine and surrounding Orange County, on the date and window you've chosen. Discreet drop-off, with or without the recipient home. We text you the moment it's delivered.

What We Ask For

When you inquire, the more you can share, the better we can design:

  • The occasion and the recipient
  • The date, delivery window, and delivery address
  • A sense of budget
  • Any color or flower preferences (or aversions)
  • Any cultural traditions we should honor
  • A photograph or two of the space, if relevant

What You Can Expect From Us

  • Same-business-day response to every inquiry
  • A written design direction and transparent quote before you commit
  • Photo confirmation of your finished arrangement before dispatch
  • Discreet, hand-delivered drop-off, with or without the recipient home
