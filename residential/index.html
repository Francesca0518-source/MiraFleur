Common questions

What's included in the $99 assessment?

Two weeks · one small floral installation in the room you choose (foyer, dining table, vanity) · our judgment on style, scale, and placement. Only two to three assessments accepted each quarter.

Do I have to subscribe after the assessment?

No. The two weeks is the test — you see what florals look like in your home, and we share our judgment on whether weekly florals fit how you live. If they don't, you walk away with that clarity.

What's the starting weekly cost?

From $159/week. Final pricing depends on the home, the number of points, the specification, and the refresh frequency — we'll discuss it openly during the assessment and present a transparent quote.

Can I just order florals once?

Yes. One-time arrangements start at $180, sized to the room and occasion. We compose, deliver, and place — no subscription required.

Can I cancel the subscription?

Three-month minimum. After that, adjust or cancel month to month with 48 hours' notice.

Can we add florals for holidays or dinners?

Yes. Before every major holiday we reach out with options for the holiday edition, quoted separately. For dinners or visiting family, mid-week refresh can be arranged on request.

Who handles the weekly delivery?

We do — at a fixed weekly window agreed with you. We'll place each arrangement in the rooms we discussed, and clear the previous week's flowers.

What's your refund policy?

Subscription changes, pauses, or cancellations require 48 hours' notice. Full terms: Service & Replacement Policy →
