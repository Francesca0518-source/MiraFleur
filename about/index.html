About Us

Mira Fleur is a bespoke floral atelier, based in Newport Coast, serving Irvine and the wider Orange County.

We started doing this to solve a real problem — in many of life's moments that genuinely matter, finding the right bouquet (or the right composition for a room) is harder than it should be.

What we offer is what three decades inside five-star hotel floral departments leaves behind: discipline, steadiness, reliability — and the judgment that only comes from doing the work, repeatedly, over a long time. We make to order. Every arrangement is shaped by the person it's for, the occasion, the season, and the space — and finished on the day of delivery.

We don't publish a standard catalog. Every commission begins with a conversation — a text message, or a request form — and ends with a piece made for that conversation alone.

An Inherited Eye

The craft behind Mira Fleur comes from three decades of training inside hotel floristry.

It's work that demands real precision — the condition of each stem, the proportion of a composition, the way colors breathe against one another, the cadence of placement and replacement. None of it stabilizes without long, repeated practice. The eye built during that time is what we bring, honestly, to every commission today.

Part of that training has its roots in the East. The Japanese ikebana sense that negative space is part of the composition; the Chinese tradition of flowers as carriers of feeling — a peony for someone beginning again, a single plum branch because the person you're sending it to will understand immediately. Both of these traditions continue to shape the way we handle a bouquet.

What truly settles after those years is less a technique than a kind of judgment — which flowers belong together, which colors should sit close to which, how much of a piece should be structure and how much should be softness. That judgment is the most concrete thing we can offer, whether the work is a single dinner-table arrangement or a long-running floral presence in a space.

Our Approach

Flowers shouldn't have a generic look.

Birthday flowers should appear made for that birthday. Memorial flowers should carry the particular quiet a family needs. Proposal flowers should carry the language two people share — not a seasonal trend.

That's why we make to order, why we begin with a brief — and why, when the flowers you're asking for aren't quite right for the moment, we'll tell you so directly.

Sourcing

We source weekly from the Los Angeles flower market and selected California specialty growers. We hold a valid California seller's permit and the relevant industry licenses.

What we can offer in any given week depends on which flowers are genuinely at their best — we'll tell you straightforwardly which stems are at peak and which aren't.
