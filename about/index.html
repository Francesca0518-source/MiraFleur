About Us

Mira Fleur is a bespoke floral atelier based in Newport Coast, serving Irvine and the wider Orange County.

We exist for one reason — that finding the right flowers for the moments that truly matter has always been harder than it should be.

What we offer is the judgment shaped over three decades of five-star and state-level hospitality floral practice — the kind of eye that develops only after handling large-scale installations, long-term contracts, and high-stakes occasions, year after year, to a single considered standard. Every commission is made to order — composed for the person, the occasion, the season, and the room it will live in, and arranged on the day of delivery.

We do not publish a standard catalogue. Every commission begins with a conversation — by message or through a request form — and ends with a piece made for that conversation alone.

Aesthetic Heritage

The judgment behind Mira Fleur was shaped over three decades inside five-star and state-level hospitality.

Those were years of refinement against an exacting standard — the condition of every stem, the proportion of every composition, the breath between colors, the rhythm of placement and replacement. Whether the work was a thousand-square-meter ballroom installation or a single arrangement bound for a private table, the standard was the same. Only the scale changed. The eye trained in those years is the eye we bring to every commission today.

The other half of this judgment comes from an Eastern sensibility of restraint and negative space — not a style, but a way of seeing: when an empty space carries more than another stem would, leaving it empty; when one bloom is enough, not adding a second. This quietness, paired with the hospitality discipline of “composure over display,” is the temperament we bring to every piece.

What truly settles after thirty years is less a technique than a sense of judgment — what flowers belong together, which colors should sit beside which, how much of a piece is structure and how much is softness. This judgment is the most honest thing we can offer a client, whether the work is a single arrangement for a dinner table or an ongoing installation in a space that is lived in.

Our Belief

Flowers should not look generic.

A birthday arrangement should look as though it was made for that particular person. A memorial arrangement should hold the kind of quiet that family needs. A proposal should carry the language only those two people share — not the seasonal trend.

This is why we work to order, and why every piece begins with a brief. It is also why, when the flowers you have in mind are not quite right for the occasion, we will tell you so.

Sourcing

Each week we source fresh stems from the Los Angeles Flower Market and select California specialty growers. We hold a valid California seller's permit and trade credentials.

What we can offer in any given week depends on what is genuinely at its best that week — and we will tell you, plainly, which flowers are in their finest condition and which are not.

If you are considering a commission, we would be glad to hear what you have in mind.
