About Mira Fleur

A private floral atelier based in Newport Coast — serving Irvine and surrounding Orange County with custom, made-to-order floral design.

The Atelier

Mira Fleur is a private floral atelier based in Newport Coast, California. We serve Irvine and surrounding Orange County with custom, made-to-order floral design — no storefront, no standing inventory, no repeated compositions. Every arrangement is built for the person, occasion, and space it's meant for.

The Aesthetic Heritage

The design sensibility behind Mira Fleur comes from three decades of five-star hotel floral work — the kind of arrangements that had to hold their own in marble lobbies, grand ballrooms, and private dining suites. That discipline — the understanding that a single stem can change the weight of a room, that restraint is often the most expensive choice — is what shapes every piece we make today.

Our Philosophy

We believe flowers should never feel generic. A birthday arrangement should look like it was made for the person whose birthday it is. A sympathy piece should carry the specific quiet a family needs. A wedding bouquet should match the bride, not the season's trend. This is why we work made-to-order, why we don't publish a standing catalogue, and why every commission starts with a conversation.

How We Source

We source fresh stems weekly from the Los Angeles Flower Market and from select specialty growers across California. We hold a valid California seller's permit and trade badge. What we can offer in any given week depends on what's genuinely beautiful that week — and we will always tell you honestly what's in peak form.

If you're considering commissioning something from us, we'd love to hear what you have in mind.
