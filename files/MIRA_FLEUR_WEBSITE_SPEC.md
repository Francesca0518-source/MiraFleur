## 1. Brand Foundation

**Brand name:** Mira Fleur
**Tagline (hero):** *Dream in bloom. Bloom in dream.*
**Positioning:** Bespoke, made-to-order floral design for Orange County and Greater Los Angeles.
**Service model:** Zero-inventory, custom-only. Every arrangement is designed and hand-crafted for the specific occasion. No standing SKUs, no warehouse.
**Aesthetic heritage:** Three decades of five-star hotel floral craftsmanship, translated into a private, personal service.
**Service area:** Newport Coast, Newport Beach, Irvine, Corona del Mar, Costa Mesa, Laguna Beach, and Greater Los Angeles.
**Business classification:** Service-area business (no retail storefront).

### Brand voice
- Quiet, confident, understated luxury. Never loud. Never discount-driven.
- Prose, not bullet points, in hero and story sections.
- Short, warm, human in functional sections (FAQ, process).
- Never uses exclamation marks in main copy. No emojis on the website.
- Avoids the word "cheap," "deal," "sale." Uses "invested," "commissioned," "thoughtful."

### Visual direction
- Palette: ivory, soft cream, ink black, muted sage, dusty rose accents.
- Typography: **Cormorant Garamond** for display/headings, **Jost** for body and UI.
- Generous whitespace. High-clarity, cool-toned photography.
- No stock photography. All imagery is real work (uploaded later by client in phases).
- Subtle fade-in animations on scroll. No parallax, no autoplay video with sound.

## 9. FAQ Page (`/faq`)

Twelve questions, grouped into three sections. This page is a critical SEO asset.

### *Ordering & Delivery*

**Q: How far in advance should I place an order?**
For custom arrangements, 48 hours is ideal, though we accommodate same-day requests for Orange County addresses when inquiries arrive before 12:00 PM PT. For weddings and large events, we recommend 6 weeks minimum; 3–6 months for peak season.

**Q: Where do you deliver?**
We deliver across Orange County — including Newport Coast, Newport Beach, Irvine, Corona del Mar, Costa Mesa, Laguna Beach, and surrounding cities — and across Greater Los Angeles, including Beverly Hills, West Hollywood, Santa Monica, and Pacific Palisades. For addresses outside this footprint, please inquire.

**Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?**
Yes, for most Orange County addresses when the inquiry and confirmation happen before 12:00 PM Pacific Time. Same-day is not guaranteed — we'll confirm availability within 30 minutes.

**Q: What is your minimum order?**
Custom arrangements start at $180. Event work begins at $2,500.

### *About the Work*

**Q: Can I request specific flowers?**
Yes, though we work with what is genuinely beautiful in any given week. If a requested flower is not in peak condition, we'll let you know and propose alternatives with the same feeling.

**Q: Will my arrangement look exactly like a photo I saw?**
No two Mira Fleur arrangements are ever identical. We design each piece individually — so while we can honor a reference photo's mood, palette, and composition, the final result will be one of a kind.

**Q: Do you handle Chinese cultural traditions and color customs?**
Yes. We are experienced with Chinese wedding, memorial, and milestone traditions, including color symbolism, appropriate flowers for each occasion, and ceremonial formats. Please mention any traditions or customs in your inquiry.

**Q: Can you work to a brief for a photoshoot or brand activation?**
Yes. We regularly collaborate with photographers, stylists, and brands. Share the brief and we'll respond with a design direction.

### *Logistics & Care*

**Q: How do I keep my arrangement fresh?**
We include a care card with every delivery. In general: keep flowers out of direct sunlight, away from air vents and fruit, change water every two days, and trim stems at an angle every few days.

**Q: Can I order flowers anonymously?**
Yes. We handle card messaging, anonymity, and delivery timing with full discretion.

**Q: Do you offer corporate accounts?**
Yes. We invoice monthly for recurring corporate clients and offer dedicated account management for brand activation and executive gifting programs.

**Q: Do you ship nationally?**
Not currently. Mira Fleur is a hand-delivered local service. Every arrangement is built on the day of delivery and driven to the recipient — shipping would compromise both.

## 14. Floating "Text Us" Button (site-wide)

A persistent floating button appears on every page to drive direct-message inquiries — the fastest conversion path for a bespoke florist.

### Placement & behavior
- **Position:** Fixed, bottom-right corner, 24px from edges on desktop, 16px from edges on mobile
- **Visible on:** All pages
- **Hidden on:** The `/inquire` page itself (redundant there)
- **Z-index:** Above all content except modals/lightboxes

### Desktop behavior
- Button shows icon (SMS bubble or lucide `MessageCircle`) + text label "Text Us"
- On hover: slight scale-up (1.05x) + subtle shadow intensify
- On click: opens a small popover card above the button showing:
  - **"Text 949-899-4022"** (formatted as clickable `sms:` link as well)
  - **"Typical response within minutes during business hours"**
  - A small "Copy number" button
  - A small "Open in Messages" link (uses `sms:` protocol — opens Messages app on Mac, Phone Link on Windows)

### Mobile behavior
- Button is a circular icon button (56px diameter), SMS bubble icon only
- On tap: directly triggers `sms:9498994022` — opens the Messages app pre-filled with the number
- Optional: include a pre-filled message body: `sms:9498994022?body=Hi Mira Fleur, I'd like to inquire about`

### Visual styling
- Background: ink black (`#1a1a1a`) or deep sage — matches brand palette
- Icon/text: cream (`#f5f1ea`)
- Border-radius: fully rounded (pill shape on desktop, circle on mobile)
- Box-shadow: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.12)`
- Font: Jost, 14px, medium weight, tracked slightly wide

### Implementation notes for Claude Code
- Implement as a single reusable component (e.g., `TextUsButton.astro`)
- Use `useMediaQuery` or CSS `@media` to switch between desktop-popover and mobile-direct-SMS behavior
- The `sms:` protocol is universally supported on iOS, Android, macOS (Messages), and Windows 11 (Phone Link)
- **Do not** use a third-party chat widget (Intercom, Tawk, Drift) — they slow the site, compromise brand aesthetic, and conflict with the bespoke positioning

### Analytics event
Fire a Google Analytics event `text_us_click` when the button is tapped or the `sms:` link is opened. This is a primary conversion metric.

## 15. Form Handling: Formspree Setup

The `/inquire` form submits to **Formspree**, which forwards every submission to the client's email inbox.

### Setup status

✅ **Formspree account is active. Form endpoint is provisioned:**

```
https://formspree.io/f/xnjldozr
```

This URL is already filled into the form HTML below. No further setup required before launch — just verify the Formspree dashboard settings listed in the next subsection.

### Client checklist (verify in Formspree dashboard before launch)

1. Confirm the email address on file in Formspree is where inquiries should be delivered (if it needs to change later, update in Formspree dashboard → Settings → Emails).
2. In Formspree dashboard settings for this form, enable:
   - **Email notifications** (on — primary delivery method)
   - **reCAPTCHA** (on — prevents bot spam)
   - **Honeypot field** (on — additional bot protection)
   - **Autoresponder** (optional — sends an automatic thank-you email to the customer; use the text in Section 10)
3. Set the email subject line in Formspree to: `🌿 New Mira Fleur Inquiry` (or customize with variable fields if Formspree plan supports it, e.g., `🌿 New Inquiry — {{name}}`).

### Form configuration in HTML

```html
<form action="https://formspree.io/f/xnjldozr" method="POST" class="inquire-form">
  <!-- fields as specified in Section 10 -->
  <input type="hidden" name="_subject" value="New Mira Fleur Inquiry">
  <input type="hidden" name="_next" value="https://mirafleur.com/inquire/thank-you">
  <input type="text" name="_gotcha" style="display:none"> <!-- honeypot -->
  <!-- ... -->
</form>
```

### Email notification format

The client will receive an email with subject "New Mira Fleur Inquiry" containing all form fields in a readable format. Reply directly from email to respond to the customer.

### Free tier limits
- Formspree free: 50 submissions/month (sufficient for early-stage)
- Formspree Basic: $10/month for 1,000 submissions + file uploads + custom redirects
- Upgrade trigger: when monthly inquiries exceed 40+ (approaching cap)

### Thank-you page
Create a simple page at `/inquire/thank-you` with the confirmation copy from Section 10. Formspree redirects successful submissions here via the `_next` hidden field.

### Making form submissions feel like SMS on your phone

Formspree sends form submissions as email. To make those emails behave like instant SMS-style notifications on the owner's phone — same bedside-table urgency, same glance-and-respond experience — configure the receiving email account with high-priority push notifications.

**Setup on iPhone:**

1. Add the receiving email account (the one registered with Formspree) to the iPhone Mail app if not already added.
2. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Mail → [the Mira Fleur email account]**
3. Enable all of:
   - **Allow Notifications** (on)
   - **Lock Screen, Notification Center, Banners** (all on)
   - **Banner Style: Persistent** (stays on screen until dismissed)
   - **Sounds** (on — pick a distinctive tone, different from the default SMS tone, so inquiries are instantly recognizable)
   - **Badges** (on)
4. Optional: mark the sender address `noreply@formspree.io` as **VIP** in the Mail app. VIP notifications bypass Focus modes and Do Not Disturb.
5. In Formspree dashboard, set the subject line to something immediately recognizable, e.g., `🌿 New Mira Fleur Inquiry — [name]` (use the form field variable for the customer name if Formspree supports it, so you can see who it's from on the lock screen).

**Setup on Android:**

1. Open the Gmail or equivalent email app.
2. Go to **Settings → [Mira Fleur email account] → Notifications**
3. Enable **All new mail** notifications with high priority.
4. Under system settings: **Settings → Apps → Gmail → Notifications** — set the notification channel for this account to **Urgent** (pops on screen, makes sound).
5. Optional: create a filter in Gmail that labels all `noreply@formspree.io` messages as `inquire` and marks them as important — then set that label to trigger priority notifications.

**Result:** When a customer submits the form, the owner's phone lights up within 2–5 seconds with a lock-screen notification showing the inquiry's subject and preview — functionally equivalent to receiving a text, but with full inquiry details one tap away.

**Why this is better than SMS forwarding:**
- Zero additional cost (vs. $20+/month for Zapier + Twilio SMS forwarding)
- Full form content is preserved (SMS is character-limited to ~160 chars)
- One-tap reply from the phone's email app, or one-tap phone-number tap-to-text to respond to the customer
- Complete audit trail in the email inbox
