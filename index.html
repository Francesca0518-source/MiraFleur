ABOUT

The Craft Behind Mira Fleur

✿

Mira Fleur

Mira Fleur was founded to bring a rarer kind of floral design to Orange County — the kind once reserved for five-star hotel lobbies and private events, now made available to the homes and celebrations of the people who live here.

The aesthetic sensibility behind the brand draws on thirty years of hospitality floral work, where every arrangement had to hold its own against marble, velvet, and chandeliers. That discipline — of proportion, of restraint, of knowing what a single stem is worth — is what shapes every piece we make today.

We don't keep inventory. We don't repeat designs. Every commission begins with a conversation and ends with something made only for you.
