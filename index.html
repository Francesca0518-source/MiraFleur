ABOUT MIRA FLEUR

Where emotion
takes form

Flowers, to us, are not decoration. They are the language we use when words come up short — for a birthday felt deeply, a thank-you that ought to land, a room that wants to be remembered.

MIRA FLEUR is a bespoke floral atelier in Newport Coast, serving Irvine and the surrounding Orange County. Each commission begins with a conversation and is shaped by season, setting, palette, and the person it is meant for. Nothing is mass-made; every piece is composed for a single moment.

Our practice is built on three decades inside five-star hotel floral departments. From that, we offer the things you cannot order from a catalogue: discipline, restraint, proportion, and the quiet beauty that stays with people long after the flowers are gone.

HOW TO ORDER

A considered process, kept simple.

01

Tell us the occasion

A birthday, a thank-you, a visit, a reception, an event, or something only you can name. Begin with a short message — we will take it from there.

02

Share a palette and a budget

Tell us the colors you love or wish to avoid, and the range you are working within. We will respond the same business day with a direction shaped by what is in season.

03

Made fresh, on the day

Once the design is approved, flowers are sourced fresh and arranged by hand on the day of delivery — never earlier. Hand-delivered across Orange County, on the window you have chosen.
