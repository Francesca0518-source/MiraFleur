ABOUT MIRA FLEUR

Where the right flowers find the right moment

Some moments call for more than words
and more than something picked off a shelf.
A bouquet, made with care, is exactly that.

A mother's birthday.
A friend's new home.
A love spoken plainly.

A surprise that lives up to her.
A thank-you that ought to land.
A goodbye left unsaid.

A hotel lobby at first light.
An evening that matters.
An office that deserves more than fluorescent calm.

For all of these, finding the right flowers —
or the right composition for a room —
is harder than it should be.

This is why Mira Fleur exists.

We are a bespoke floral atelier in Newport Coast,
serving Irvine and the wider Orange County.
Every commission begins with a conversation —
shaped by the person, the occasion, the season,
and the room the flowers will live in.

Each piece is composed for the place it is going,
to arrive at its fullest and to hold that fullness
as long as the season allows.

Flowers are sourced fresh and arranged by hand
on the day of delivery. Never the day before.
It is the most basic respect we can offer a stem,
and our quiet promise that nothing tired
will ever arrive at your door.

The hands behind this work were trained
across three decades inside five-star hotel floral departments.
That training brings what a chain florist cannot —
restraint, proportion, a sense of where each stem belongs,
and the instinct to read what a room is asking for:
something soft, something composed, something grand,
something still, something alight —
or anything that lives between, and is exactly what the moment needs.

HOW TO ORDER

A considered process, kept simple.

01

Tell us the occasion

A birthday, a thank-you, a visit, a reception, an event, or something only you can name. Begin with a short message — we will take it from there.

02

Share a palette and a budget

Tell us the colors you love or wish to avoid, and the range you are working within. We will respond the same business day with a direction shaped by what is in season.

03

Made fresh, on the day

Once the design is approved, flowers are sourced fresh and arranged by hand on the day of delivery — never earlier. Hand-delivered across Orange County, on the window you have chosen.
