ABOUT

The Craft Behind Mira Fleur

✿

Mira Fleur

Mira Fleur began with a simple observation: for many of the moments that matter most, the right flower is harder to find than it should be. We started this work to fix that — for the people who live here, in the homes and the celebrations that ask for something more careful.

What we offer comes from three decades inside five-star hotel floral departments — the discipline, the reliability, the long apprenticeship in proportion and color that lets us know, fairly quickly, what a particular flower can do for a particular room. That training is the honest part of what we bring to every commission.

Every commission begins with a conversation, by text or by request form, and ends with a piece designed for that conversation alone.
