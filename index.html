OUR STORY

About Mira Fleur

Mira Fleur is rooted in three decades of five-star hotel floral artistry — where every stem is placed with intention, and every space becomes a silent conversation.

Today, we bring that standard of beauty to Irvine, Newport Coast, and every moment across Southern California that deserves to be treated with care. Whether it's a personal gift that speaks from the heart, or a tablescape that sets the tone for an evening, Mira Fleur upholds hotel-level quality in every detail.

Style without limits, beauty with standards — that is our promise for every piece we create.
