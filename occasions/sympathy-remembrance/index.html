Sympathy & Remembrance Flowers in Orange County

In the hardest moments, the right flowers say what words cannot. Mira Fleur creates sympathy arrangements, memorial wreaths, and remembrance florals with the restraint and dignity these occasions deserve. We deliver to homes, funeral services, places of worship, and memorial gatherings across Irvine and surrounding Orange County. Our sympathy work draws on traditions from many cultures — Western, Chinese, and others — and we are careful to honor the particular customs of each family. If you tell us the family's background, we will design accordingly, with the appropriate flowers, palette, and form.

We approach this work quietly. No upselling, no fanfare — just careful, respectful design.

Frequently Asked

Can you deliver directly to a funeral home or service?

Yes. Please share the funeral home name, address, and the time of service at least 4 hours before.

Do you design for Chinese memorial customs specifically?

Yes. We are experienced with traditional Chinese funeral florals, including appropriate white and yellow palettes, chrysanthemums, and wreath formats. Please mention this in your inquiry.

Can you be discreet about the sender?

Yes. We handle card messaging, anonymity, and delivery timing with full discretion.
