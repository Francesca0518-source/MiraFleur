Spaces & Business Occasions

Florals in a business setting aren't decoration — they signal the kind of company that notices details. We work with businesses in two ways. The first is ongoing: a long-term relationship with a single space, where florals are part of how the room reads. The second is occasional: a dinner, a board meeting, a quiet conference room that needs a centerpiece for the day.

Ongoing · Limited to 2–3 spaces per quarter

Mira Space Florals

Hotel lobbies, executive offices, galleries, and retail interiors. Weekly or biweekly florals — front-of-house pieces, reception arrangements, or full-room compositions — designed in conversation with the architecture and the people who pass through it.

Every space partnership begins with a Mira Space Preview: a two-week observation, a site visit, and a written report on palette, scale, cadence, and a recommended long-term direction. From there, you decide whether to proceed. Standing engagements begin at one quarter (three months); to keep the work attentive, we accept only two to three new space partners per quarter.

Business Dinners, Boards & Conferences

A single evening, a single room. Centerpieces for a private client dinner, a quiet arrangement on a boardroom table, low florals down a long conference setup. We arrive, place, and return for vase exchange the next morning if needed.

Designed and quoted per event. We're based in Irvine and surrounding Orange County; for larger installations we travel farther — including Los Angeles and eastern OC — case-by-case.
