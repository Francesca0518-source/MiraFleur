Wedding & Proposal Florals in Orange County

From intimate at-home proposals to full-scale wedding installations, Mira Fleur designs florals for the days you will remember for the rest of your life. We work with couples across Irvine, Newport Beach, Newport Coast, Tustin, Costa Mesa, and Laguna Niguel to create bouquets, ceremony arches, reception tablescapes, and every considered detail in between. For full-day weddings and large installations, we travel beyond Orange County — Los Angeles and farther — case-by-case. Because we operate on a made-to-order model, no two weddings we design ever look alike — yours will be built around your venue, your palette, and the feeling you want your guests to step into.

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should we book our wedding florals?

For peak season (May–October), we recommend booking at least 3 months in advance. For intimate weddings or proposals, 2–4 weeks is often workable.

Do you offer in-person consultations?

Yes. Consultations are by appointment at our Newport Coast atelier or at your venue.

Do you set up and tear down on the day?

Yes. Full-service setup and breakdown is included in all event packages.
