Frequently Asked

How far in advance should we book?

For intimate weddings, ceremonies, and larger proposal pieces, 3–6 weeks is preferred; longer for peak season (May–October). Last-minute proposals (under a week) are sometimes possible — text us to ask.

Do you do full-scale weddings?

Not at this time. We focus on the pieces a couple wears, holds, and sits beside — bouquets, sweetheart tables, ceremony details — and on intimate ceremonies. For full-venue installations, we're glad to refer trusted partners.

How do consultations work?

By text, by email, or by phone call once we've seen your brief. We don't currently host in-person consultations.
